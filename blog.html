Neuropsycholgy Basics

Neuropsychologists use psychological measurement to assess brain functioning

They can diagnose

  • Dementia
  • Intellectual Impairment
  • ADHD
  • Autism
  • Brain Damage
  • And More

How testing is done

Neuropsychologists use the help of psychometrists who run patients through various cognitive tests. Each test has been thoroughly researched in order to gather enough test samples to know what is considered normal preformance versus impaired preformance. By finding which cognitive systems are impaired, neuropsychologists can reach a conclusion of what is happening in the brain.
